Norl***** R

Light Fit Cushion SPF33 PA++

very natural finish, stays on pretty long, sweat-proof, decent coverage

this product has a semi-matte finish and i think it looks pretty natural (if you want something matte or dewy, they have other cushion ranges for you to choose ) i’m using N23 and it matches my skin tone super well coverage-wise, it’s not super heavy and i like it that way. I have quite a few acne scars, uneven skin tone, redness in some areas of my skin and occasionally pimples. it only partially covers my acne scars, even after multiple layers, so you probably need something thicker - maybe foundation or throw in concealer before using this. it helps to balance out the redness and uneven skin tone pretty well. it also lightens my under eye circles by ~70-80% without concealer so that’s great i could probably get more coverage with foundation but i prefer using this over foundation because it’s super easy and quick to apply! it’s very light so it’s quite blendable. very beginner friendly :) i’ve tried using other foundations before but i’m not experienced with makeup so the foundation tends to become really patchy and streaky. i don’t wear makeup often so i’m also not used to thick layers of foundation. i find them too uncomfortable and i can feel a layer sitting on top my skin. this one is so lightweight and breathable, i don’t realised i’m wearing makeup so i love it!! also, the staying power is pretty good!! downside is, you need to give it about 20minutes to set otherwise it will transfer, especially now that we wear masks. but after about 20min, it stays on for ~8h (i haven’t worn it for longer than that). i’ve also done a workout with it and it is sweatproof !!! i also love how it’s not smudgeable. i avoided wearing makeup in the past because i have a bad habit of touching my face and that would mess up my makeup but this foundation stays put :) there’s also no pilling! for the price, i would say this is a steal! it didn’t make me break out and the only flaw would be the setting time. i need to wait 20min before i can put on my mask and leave the house. would recommend if you have mild to moderate blemishes! if you have very dark scars, the coverage for this product won’t be enough for you 10/10
